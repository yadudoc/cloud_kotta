Managing Jobs¶

Cloud Kotta makes it easy to keep track of your jobs by maintaining complete history of your jobs. The Previous Jobs tab allows you to view this history. The following sections cover the different modes of interaction possible.

Job Status¶

Jobs have the following states:

  • Pending : Jobs are in pending state when they are waiting in queue for execution. While wait times are generally under 5 minutes, depending on the configuration of the pool you are submitting to, wait times can vary.
  • Staging In : The job has been launched on a compute node, and the input files are being fetched from remote storage to the local disks.
  • Processing : The job is being executed on the compute node.
  • Staging Out : The job has completed execution and the output files are being transferred from local disk to remote storage.
  • Failed : The job has completed execution with failures.
  • Completed : The job has completed execution without failures.
